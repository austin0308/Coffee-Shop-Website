Autobiography

Founded in 2006 by MSU graduate students Michel Bohmer and Sandy T homas. Located in the heart of MSU’s campus town, Savanna’s is designed to be a funky destination. We serve premium products such as fresh roasted coffee and espresso drinks, hot and cold teas, grilled and cold sandwiches, and homemade soups. Not just a daytime destination! Stop by in the evening, enjoy a glass of wine or beer and listen to some live entertainment in our connected theatre space!
